Campus Building

Fermat Building

← Back to homepage

The Fermat building is one of the CY Tech satellite buildings used for practical work (TP), computer labs, and small-group sessions. It is part of the broader CY Tech campus network in Cergy. You may have sessions scheduled here throughout the year, particularly for Computer Science and Engineering modules.

The building is equipped with workstations, lab benches, and dedicated project rooms. It is generally open during scheduled class hours. Always check your timetable to confirm which building your sessions are in — it is sometimes Fermat and sometimes the Site du Parc.

🎓 Main use
TP sessions, computer labs, group work
💻 Equipment
Workstations, lab benches, project rooms

CY Tech campus area, Cergy
