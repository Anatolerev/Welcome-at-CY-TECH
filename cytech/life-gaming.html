Student Association

Gaming Club

← Back to homepage

The CY Tech Gaming Club is one of the most popular student associations on campus. Sessions take place regularly in the student lounge with high-performance gaming PC. The club organises LAN parties, esports tournaments, and evening sessions throughout the year. Whether you are a casual gamer or a competitive player, you are welcome — no sign-up required.

The club competes in inter-school tournaments against ESSEC, ENSEA, and other Cergy schools. They also organise themed events (Valorant nights, FIFA tournaments, Smash Bros sessions) at the start of each semester. Check the BDE board or Instagram for the schedule.

LoL, CS2, Valorant, Rocket League, FIFA...
