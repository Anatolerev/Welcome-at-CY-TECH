University Building

Les Chênes — CY Université

← Back to homepage

The Les Chênes building is the iconic main building of CY Cergy Paris Université and one of the most recognisable landmarks in Cergy. It houses the central university administration, the main library, student services, and several lecture halls. All CY Tech students have full access to the library and its extensive resources.

The library offers thousands of scientific journals, books, and online databases. It has dedicated study spaces, computers, printers, and free high-speed wifi. The building has been awarded the "Services Publics +" quality label for the excellence of its services. If you need a quiet place to work or access resources beyond what's on the Site du Parc, this is the place to go.
