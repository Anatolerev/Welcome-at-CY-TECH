Student Association

CY Poker Club

← Back to homepage

The CY Poker Club is a student association that brings together poker enthusiasts from across the campus. The club organises regular evening sessions open to all levels, as well as larger tournaments between different groups.

Games are played with chips only — no real money involved. It is also a great social activity and an excellent way to meet students from different years and programs. The atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming. Look out for the sign-up sheet at the start of the year near the BDE office.

🃏 Format
Texas Hold'em — chips only, no real money
📅 Sessions
Every Wednesaday — check BDE calendar for more
🏆 Tournaments
Competitions, campus leagues
🎓 Level
All levels — complete beginners welcome
💰 Cost
Free to join
📍 Location
Cauchy 211, Parc's Site — check BDE for details
