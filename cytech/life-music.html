Student Association

L'Art Scène — Music Club

← Back to homepage

L'Art Scène is the official music association of CY Tech. It brings together students who love music — whether as performers or audience members. The association is open to all instruments and all genres: rock, jazz, pop, electronic, classical, and more. You do not need to be a professional musician to join — all levels are welcome.

The association organises the Welcome Concert at the start of each academic year, held at Théâtre 95 in Cergy (2 Place du Théâtre). It is one of the biggest student events of the year. Throughout the year, L'Art Scène also runs regular jam sessions and open mic nights on campus. If you want to perform or just enjoy live music with other students, this is the place.
