What's Available

Types of opportunities

From your first year to graduation, here is what CY Tech makes possible.

🌍

Semester Abroad

Spend one or two semesters at one of 76 partner universities worldwide. You keep your CY Tech enrollment and the credits count towards your degree. Apply through the international relations office.

From Year 3
🏢

Internship (Stage)

Multiple mandatory internships throughout the degree. Each year includes at least one placement in a company — from 4 weeks in prep year to 6+ months in the engineering cycle.

Every Year
🔄

Apprenticeship (Alternance)

Work-study contract available in CS and Applied Maths from Year 3. You alternate between 4 weeks at school and 4 weeks in a company, while being paid a salary.

From Year 3
🎓

Double Degree

Get two degrees at the same time. CY Tech partners with ESSEC Business School and Grenoble EM, allowing engineering students to graduate with both an engineering degree and a management diploma.

Year 4-5
🔬

Research & PhD

After graduating, CY Tech students can pursue a doctorate at CY Cergy Paris Université or partner institutions. Research labs on campus cover AI, applied mathematics, embedded systems, and more.

Post-grad
✈️

VIE — Work Abroad

The Volontariat International en Entreprise (VIE) lets recent graduates work for a French company abroad for 6 to 24 months. Popular destination: Germany, UK, Canada, Japan.

Post-grad
