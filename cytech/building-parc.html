Campus Building

Parc's Site

← Back to homepage

The Parc's Site is the main campus of CY Tech and the heart of your academic life. This is where the majority of your classes will take place, especially if you study Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Finance or Industrial Engineering. The building is open from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. for enrolled students, and computers are available on-site at all times.

You will also find the student services office, the BDE (Bureau des Étudiants — student union), and most of the administrative departments here. This is your go-to building for any administrative question, course registration, or student association activity.
