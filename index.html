Around Cergy

What to do nearby

Cergy is a lively student city. Here are the top spots close to the campus.

🛍️

Les 3 Fontaines

One of the largest shopping centres in Île-de-France. 150+ stores, cinema (Kinepolis), restaurants and food court.

📍 10 min on foot
🏊

Aquatic Centre

Modern public swimming pool with lap lanes, leisure pool and wellness area. Student rates available.

📍 15 min by bus
⚽

Stade du Ponceau

Home of FC Cergy-Pontoise. Watch local matches or use the adjacent training pitches for student football.

📍 20 min on foot
🏖️

Leisure Island

Sandy beaches, kayak, paddle, climbing wall, bike trails. Free entry. Open in summer.

📍 RER A — Cergy-le-Haut
🎭

Théâtre 95

Main concert and theatre venue. Student discounts. Home of the CY Tech welcome concert every October.

📍 2 Place du Théâtre, Cergy
☕

Cergy Port

Lively riverside neighbourhood with cafés, bars, restaurants. Perfect for after-class meetups.

📍 15 min on foot

Practical Info

Everything you need to know

Transport, housing, food, admin — the essentials to start on the right foot.

🚆

Getting around — RER A

Paris (Châtelet) in ~45 min. Stations: Cergy-Préfecture, Saint-Christophe, Cergy-le-Haut. Navigo monthly ~€86. → iledefrance-mobilites.fr

🏠

Student Housing (CROUS)

From €274/month, all utilities included. Apply via DSE between January and May. → messervices.etudiant.gouv.fr

🍽️

University Restaurant

€3.30/meal — €1 with scholarship. Mon–Fri 11:30am–1:30pm.

💰

Scholarship (Bourse CROUS)

Apply via DSE based on family income. Grants access to €1 meals and priority housing. → etudiant.gouv.fr

🏥

Health — Sécurité Sociale

All students are automatically covered. Update your Carte Vitale. For complementary insurance → ameli.fr

📋

CVEC — Mandatory fee

~€100 to pay before enrolment. Required for registration. Pay online at cvec.etudiant.gouv.fr
