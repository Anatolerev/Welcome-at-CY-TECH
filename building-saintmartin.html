Graduate Campus

Saint-Martin Site

← Back to homepage

The Saint-Martin site is a striking modern glass building that hosts advanced research activities, graduate programs, and Master's level courses. With its distinctive architecture, it is one of the most impressive buildings in the Cergy area. If you pursue a Master's degree or engage in research projects later in your studies, this is where much of that work will take place.

The building is home to research laboratories, seminar rooms, and thesis supervision offices. It also hosts events, conferences, and workshops open to the broader student community. As a first or second-year student, you may not have many classes here yet — but it is the destination you are working towards.
